# Recipe Adjuster Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Goals and Background Context

### Goals

- Enable home cooks to discover recipes based on available ingredients, reducing food waste by 25% within 30 days of use
- Provide AI-powered ingredient substitution suggestions with explanations, achieving 70%+ user helpfulness ratings
- Deliver offline-first PWA experience with instant load times and full functionality without internet connection
- Build community-driven recipe database reaching 500 contributed recipes within 6 months of launch
- Achieve 1,000 active users within 3 months and 10,000 within 6 months through organic growth
- Operate within zero-budget constraint using free-tier infrastructure for first 6 months
- Maintain 99% uptime and <2 second response time for AI substitution queries
- Empower cooking confidence through educational content explaining cooking principles and substitution chemistry

### Background Context

Recipe Adjuster addresses a critical problem facing home cooks globally: inefficient use of available ingredients leading to food waste ($1,500-2,000 annually per household), unnecessary grocery spending, and decision fatigue. Existing recipe apps focus on cuisine-based discovery rather than ingredient optimization, and those offering ingredient-based search lack intelligent substitution guidance, offline functionality, and educational value.

The convergence of rising food costs (20% increase 2020-2024), growing sustainability awareness among Gen Z and Millennials, and AI technology maturation (Spring AI, local LLMs) creates a unique market opportunity. With 82% of meals now prepared at home post-pandemic, users are primed for a solution combining ingredient optimization with AI-powered cooking assistance.
